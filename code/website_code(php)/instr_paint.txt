In this section, you will be presented with one emotion (such as happiness) at a time, along with the pictures of two blank human bodies. <strong>For each emotion presented, think carefully what you feel in your body when you experience the emotion. Then, color the body regions where you feel your bodily activities change when experiencing the emotion (in this example, happiness)</strong>. 
<br>
<br>
On the <strong>left blank body</strong>, color the regions where you feel the activity <strong>increasing or getting stronger</strong>. 
<br>
On the <strong>right blank body</strong>, color the regions where you feel the activity <strong>decreasing or getting weaker</strong>.
<br>
You can color any region of the bodies you feel appropriate, from head to toes.
<br>
<br>
You will be asked to provide 7 evaluations in total, for 6 emotions and a neutral state.
